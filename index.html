Petropavl or Petropavlovsk is a city on the Ishim River in northern Kazakhstan close to the border with Russia. It is the capital of the North Kazakhstan Region.
The city is also known colloquially in Kazakh as Kyzylzhar.

    General characteristics:
  • Population: 218,956
  • Area: 224.9 km²
  • Founded: July 12, 1752
  • Elevation: 140 m
